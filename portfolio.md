## Research

### In-Situ Control of Wire-Arc Additive Manufacturing
*RPI · Mishra Lab · 2025–Present*

![WAAM Project](assets/img/portfolio/waam.jpg)

Investigating real-time control of material properties and bead geometry during the Wire-Arc Additive Manufacturing (WAAM) process using robotic manipulators. The goal is to close the loop on process variables — deposition rate, heat input, and path planning — to produce parts with consistent mechanical properties without post-process inspection.

**Topics:** Model Predictive Control · Robotic Path Planning · Process Monitoring · Sensor Fusion

---

### Aerodynamics-Aware Control of Tailsitter Vehicles
*RPI · Mishra Lab · 2023–2024*

![Tailsitter Project](assets/img/portfolio/tailsitter.gif)

Contributed to the design and analysis of controllers for tailsitter UAVs that explicitly account for aerodynamic effects across the full flight envelope — hover, transition, and forward flight. This work was published in the 2024 IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics.

**Authors:** Kristoff Macintosh, Jayden Smith, Sandipan Mishra  
**Topics:** UAV Control · Aerodynamics · Trajectory Optimization

[📄 Read the Paper](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10557463)
